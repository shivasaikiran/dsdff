About Us

It would be true to say that XCross has changed the way Indians cook today. The first to introduce the concept of modular kitchen in India, XCross has consistently innovated and refined its products to offer the best to the Indian consumer.The company started its manufacturing process with wire accessories, and soon added Stainless Steel accessories to its product portfolio under the brand name XCross.

Today, XCross stands tall as a complete kitchen solutions provider. In 2016, XCross aligned forces with India's largest Kitchen accessories company SML Industries. With this association, XCross is looking forward to unprecedented growth in the coming years. Today, XCross boasts of the largest network of kitchen dealers based all over India.
